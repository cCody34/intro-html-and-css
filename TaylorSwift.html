Re-recordings (Taylor's Version)

Taylor Swift has re-recorded 3 of her original 10 studio albums, this is due to a dispute with record executive and investor Scooter Braun. For more information about why Taylor is re-recording her albums click here.

Taylor's re-recording project has been extremely successful, with Red (Taylor's Version) winning one of its three Grammy nominations.
